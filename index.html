Welcome to Marvelous Africa Training Center

Africa's premier hands-on vocational training center — equipping workers, artisans, and professionals with industry-ready skills in Heavy Equipment Operation, Electrical & Electronics, Fashion Design, Welding and Fabrication

What we do

Dedicated to providing world-class vocational and technical training

At Marvelous Africa Training Center, we believe that practical skills are the most powerful tool for transforming lives. Based in Accra Ghana and Lagos Nigeria, we are dedicated to providing world-class vocational and technical training that prepares our students for real-world employment and entrepreneurship. Whether you are looking to operate heavy construction machinery, master electrical and electronics installations, or launch a career in fashion design, we have the right program for you. Our certified instructors, hands-on training environment, and strong industry connections ensure that every student graduates work-ready and confident. Your skill is your wealth — and we are here to help you build it.

Why Choose Us?

Certified Instructors

Learn from experienced, industry-certified professionals who bring real-world knowledge into the classroom and training field.

100% Hands-On Training

We don't just teach theory — our students get direct, practical experience operating equipment, handling tools, and executing real projects from day one.

Nationally Acceptable Certificate

Graduate with a recognized certificate that opens doors to jobs, contracts, and opportunities across Nigeria and beyond.

Job Placement Support

Our Training Programs

Heavy Equipment Operations Training

Operate bulldozers, excavators, rollers, cranes, and more. Get certified and step into Ghana and Nigeria's booming construction industry.

Electrical & Electronics Training

Master CCTV installation, air conditioning setup, mobile phone repairs, electrical installation, electronics repair and more.

Fashion Designing School

Unleash your creativity. Learn cutting, sewing, pattern drafting, and garment construction from skilled fashion instructors.

Welding & Fabrication Training

Learn arc welding, MIG/TIG welding, and metal fabrication skills that are in high demand across construction, manufacturing, and industrial sectors.
