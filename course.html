Heavy Equipment Operations Training

Courses Include
  • Bulldozer Operation
  • Excavator Operation
  • Roller / Compactor Operation
  • Crane Operation
  • Loader Operation
  • Forklift Operation
  • Grader Operation
Overview

The construction industry in Nigeria is one of the fastest-growing sectors — and skilled equipment operators are in massive demand. Our Heavy Equipment Operations Training program prepares you to safely and efficiently operate a wide range of construction machinery used on commercial, industrial, and government projects.

What you will learn
  • Safe operation and handling of heavy construction equipment
  • Equipment inspection, pre-start checks, and basic maintenance
  • Site safety regulations and standards
  • Practical operation on real machines in a controlled environment
  • Reading and interpreting basic construction site plans
Who is this for?

This program is ideal for working adults seeking to enter the construction industry, current site workers looking to upgrade their skills, and individuals seeking employment with construction companies, government projects, or oil and gas firms.

Electrical & Electronics Training Program

Courses Include
  • CCTV Installation & Configuration
  • Air Conditioning (A/C) Installation & Maintenance
  • Mobile Phone Repair & Diagnostics
  • Home & Commercial Electrical Wiring
  • Solar Panel Installation & Maintenance
  • POS Machine & Small Electronics Repair
Overview

From smart homes to commercial buildings, the demand for skilled electrical and electronics technicians continues to grow across Lagos and all of Nigeria. Our Electrical & Electronics Training Program covers the most in-demand specializations, equipping students with the practical skills to work independently or as part of a larger team.

What you will learn
  • Installation, wiring, and configuration of CCTV systems
  • A/C unit installation, servicing, and fault diagnosis
  • Mobile phone hardware and software troubleshooting and repair
  • Electrical safety standards and best practices
  • How to set up and grow your own electrical/electronics business
Who is this for?

This program is ideal for anyone who wants a practical, in-demand trade skill — whether to gain employment, become self-employed, or add technical capability to an existing business.

Fashion Designing School

Courses Include
  • Pattern Making & Drafting
  • Cutting & Sewing Techniques
  • Men's Wear Design & Construction
  • Fabric Selection & Textile Knowledge
  • Fashion Illustration & Design Concepts
  • Starting & Running a Fashion Business
Overview

Nigeria's fashion industry is one of the most vibrant and fast-growing in Africa. At Marvelous Africa Training Center's Fashion Designing School, we train the next generation of skilled fashion designers and tailors — from beginners to advanced creators. Whether you want to start your own fashion brand, work with designers, or simply master the craft, this is your starting point.

What you will learn
  • Foundational and advanced sewing and construction techniques
  • How to create garments from scratch using professional patterns
  • Fabric handling, measurement, and tailoring skills
  • How to design, produce, and present finished fashion pieces
  • Business skills for launching your own fashion label or studio
Who is this for?

This program is perfect for aspiring fashion designers, tailors seeking to advance their skills, creative individuals who want to turn their passion into a profession, and entrepreneurs looking to enter the fashion industry.

Welding & Fabrication Training

Courses Include
  • Arc Welding (MMA/SMAW)
  • Structural Steel Fabrication
  • TIG Welding (Tungsten Inert Gas)
  • Oxy-Acetylene Welding & Cutting
  • Sheet Metal Fabrication
  • Welding Safety & Quality Control
Overview

Welding and metal fabrication are among the most sought-after trade skills in Nigeria's construction, manufacturing, oil and gas, and industrial sectors. At Marvelous Africa Training Center, our Welding & Fabrication Training program equips students with the technical skills to work safely and professionally with metal — from basic joints to complex fabricated structures.

What you will learn
  • Fundamental and advanced welding techniques across multiple processes
  • Metal cutting, joining, and shaping using professional equipment
  • Reading and interpreting fabrication drawings and weld symbols
  • Health and safety standards for welding environments
  • How to start and run a welding and fabrication business
Who is this for?

This program is ideal for working adults seeking a high-paying trade skill, individuals looking to enter the construction, manufacturing, or oil and gas industries, and entrepreneurs who want to set up their own welding and fabrication workshop.

Certification

Graduates receive a Nationally Acceptable Certificate in their enrolled program upon successful completion of training and assessment.
