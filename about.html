About Us

We're Marvelous Africa Training Center

Marvelous Africa Training Center is a leading vocational and technical skills institution with locations in Accra, Ghana and Lagos, Nigeria. We were founded with a clear and powerful mission — to bridge the skills gap in Africa's workforce by providing practical, affordable, and high-quality training programs that empower individuals to earn a living and build sustainable livelihoods.

Who we are

Marvelous training center is a Ghanaian owned certified vocational training center established to provide the right and required skill-set to be gainfully employed. Marvelous training center was established in September 2020 by Mr. Alhassan Adams, a youth activist and Entrepreneur to provide high quality practical vocational and technical skills training to the general public no matter their Political, Social, Gender, Religious or Educational background. We believe everyone can get trained in TVET programs. Our tailor made programs are designed also for very busy people who have the desire to learn something new or people who want to change careers. Our mission is to equip our youth with employable skills training.

Vision

To become Africa's most trusted vocational training institution — producing a generation of skilled, self-reliant, and globally competitive professionals.

Mission

To deliver world-class, hands-on vocational training that equips individuals with the skills, certification, and confidence needed to thrive in today's competitive job market.
